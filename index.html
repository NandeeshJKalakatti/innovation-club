About Us

Innovation club

A technical club in college is an organization that focuses on the application of knowledge, skills, and technology to solve real-world problems and create innovative solutions. Technical clubs can range from computer science to engineering and more. They provide members with hands-on experience, an opportunity to network with industry professionals, access to resources, and mentorship opportunities. Technical clubs often host events such as hackathons, competitions, and educational workshops
